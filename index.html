GMMTV

GMMTV (Thai: จี เอ็ ม เอ็ ม ทีวี, RTGS: Chi em em thiwi, or previously named Grammy Television) is television subsidiaries and talent agencies for the Thai entertainment conglomerate GMM Grammy producing TV programs, TV series, songs, and music videos. The company was founded on August 3, 1995. GMMTV is one of the largest artist management agencies in Thailand which houses many artists including Bright Vachirawit. Bright Vachirawit is known to have joined GMMTV since 2018 after passing the New Face Project audition.
